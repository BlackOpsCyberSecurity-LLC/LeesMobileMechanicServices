Why Choose Us?

Lee’s Mobile Mechanic Service provides fast, reliable, and affordable automotive repair directly at your home, job site, or roadside. I handle everything from diagnostics and routine maintenance to emergency breakdown repairs, saving you the hassle of towing or waiting at a shop. With professional tools and years of hands-on experience, I deliver honest, dependable work you can count on — wherever you are.

Comprehensive Service List (pricing varies by vehicle make, model, and year):
  • Diagnostics & Electronics: Full vehicle diagnostics, check engine light troubleshooting, pre-purchase inspections, electrical system diagnostics, starting & charging system repair, battery testing & replacement, ECU/computer diagnostics & programming, sensor replacement, wiring & harness repair.
  • Engine Services: Oil changes, tune-ups & spark plugs, belts & pulleys, timing belt/chain replacement, cooling system repair, alternator & starter replacement, fuel system repair, engine overhaul, head gasket replacement, air intake & throttle body cleaning.
  • Transmission & Drivetrain: Automatic & manual transmission service, clutch repair, differential & transfer case repair, CV joints & driveshaft repair, wheel bearings replacement.
  • Brakes & Suspension: Brake pads, rotors, calipers, brake fluid flush, brake lines & hoses, shocks & struts, suspension components, steering system repair, alignment checks & adjustments.
  • Cooling & HVAC: Radiator repair/replacement, hoses & coolant flush, heater core repair, AC recharge & troubleshooting, compressor replacement, condenser & evaporator repair.
  • Tires & Wheels: Tire replacement & rotation, flat tire repair, wheel balancing, alignment.
  • Exhaust & Emissions: Muffler, catalytic converter, piping replacement, oxygen sensor replacement, exhaust manifold repair.
  • Roadside & Emergency: Jump-starts, battery replacement, lockouts, tire changes, emergency diagnostics, towing assistance coordination.
  • Maintenance & Fluids: Fluids top-off & flushes, air filter & cabin filter replacement, fuel system cleaning, wiper blade replacement, light bulbs replacement.
  • Miscellaneous: Trailer hitch installation, accessories installation, vehicle inspection & safety checks, preventive maintenance packages.
